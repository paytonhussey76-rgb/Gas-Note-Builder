Gas Note Builder

A tap-based tool built for gas locate crews to fill out completion notes fast — no typing, no memorizing shortcut codes, works offline, and installs to your home screen like a real app.

Live app: https://paytonhussey76-rgb.github.io/Gas-Note-Builder/


What is this?

Filling out completion forms in the field is repetitive: pipe sizes, materials, casing, whether it's a service line or a mainline, notes about meters and risers — the same handful of phrases over and over. This app turns that into a few taps instead of typing it all out each time, then hands you a finished note ready to paste into your ticket.

It's a single self-contained web app — no install from an app store, no account, no login. Open the link, and everything runs right in your browser.

Features


Note Builder — tap pipe sizes for Poly, Steel, and High Pressure to build a note live. Tap a size again to mark multiple. Poly has its own "in casing" section so a plain and a cased line of the same size can both be marked at once. Any High Pressure pick automatically adds the standby locator safety reminder. There's also an Abandoned section, marked "by measurement."
Quick Copy — save your most common full notes as presets. Tap one and it's copied to your clipboard, ready to paste. Add, reorder, and remove your own.
Project Houses — on multi-hookup jobs, tap "Find nearby houses" to pull nearby addresses by GPS, grouped by street. Tap the ones you hooked up to and it builds a "Hooked up at ___" note automatically — with an option to merge it with whatever's in Note Builder into one combined note.
Works offline — once loaded, the app keeps working with no signal (Project Houses' address lookup still needs a connection).
Installable — add it to your home screen and it opens full-screen like a normal app.
Built-in Help — tap the ? in the top right for a guide to every section, an install walkthrough for your specific phone and browser, a "What's New" changelog, and a link to report issues.


Getting started


Open the live app link above.
Tap the ? in the top right → Install as an App for step-by-step instructions for your phone.
Everything else — sizes, presets, saved houses — is explained in the same Help menu.


Your custom sizes, Quick Copy presets, and in-progress notes are saved automatically on your device. They're personal to your phone, not shared with the rest of the crew.
